Quantum Computing · Novel Research

PFQVS — Perlin-Fourier Quantum State Simulation

Novel technique using Perlin noise seeded with environmental derivatives (humidity, temperature, time) as a true-randomness approximation for quantum state simulation. More resource-efficient than IBM's queued quantum computers for small-to-medium tasks. In preparation for academic submission.
