Investigative nonfiction examining the relationship between industrial fluoride production, corporate suppression of public health whistleblowers, and the history of fluoridation in US drinking water. Academic Papers

All three papers published to Academia.edu in September 2024 — the same month.

2024

Academic Paper · Academia.edu · September 2024

The Significance of Ziyarah in Shia Islam: A Theological and Historical Analysis

30-page theological and historical analysis arguing for the legitimacy of Shia pilgrimage practices (ziyarah) against Sunni objections. Examines primary hadith sources, classical jurisprudence, and the theological distinction between intercession and worship. Published at age 15 under legal name Amin Alogaili.

2024

Academic Paper · Academia.edu · September 2024

Hygienic Implications of the Hadith Regarding Fly Dipping

Research proposal applying modern microbiology to a classical Sunni hadith (narrated by Abu Huraira) concerning the submersion of a fly in liquid. Examines microbial contamination potential, the possibility of pathogen neutralization via submersion, and microbial load distribution across fly anatomy. Published September 2024.

2024

Academic Paper · Academia.edu · September 2024

Quantum Computing Curriculum for High School Students

Educational design paper introducing qubits, superposition, quantum gates, entanglement, and Grover's Search algorithm to secondary school students. Proposes a structured curriculum sequence and pedagogical approach for bringing quantum computing to pre-college education. Informed by Harvard's online Quantum Mechanics I & II and Quantum Computing with Qiskit courses.
