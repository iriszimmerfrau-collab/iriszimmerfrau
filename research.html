Quantum Computing · Novel Simulation Method

PFQVS — Perlin-Fourier Quantum State Simulation

Novel technique using Perlin noise seeded with environmental derivatives (humidity, temperature, time) as a true-randomness approximation for quantum state simulation. More resource-efficient than IBM's queued quantum computers for small-to-medium tasks. Performance benchmarks compiled; in preparation for academic submission. Built on the amin-qvm library.

In Submission

Quantum · Machine Learning · Hybrid Systems

Quantum-Classical Hybrid ML Experiment

amin-qvm as the quantum computation layer alongside a classical neural network. Benchmarks quantum vs. classical performance on structured tasks. Potentially the first publicly documented quantum-classical hybrid built by a solo developer under 18.

AI · Retrieval-Augmented Generation · Evaluation Infrastructure

RAG Pipeline with Eval Harness

Retrieval-augmented generation over a real Arabic corpus (Quranic text, classical poetry). Includes a custom evaluation layer tracking retrieval precision, answer faithfulness, and hallucination rate. Deployed via FastAPI and Docker with structured logging and latency dashboards. DECISIONS.md included to document all architectural tradeoffs.

Planned

NLP · Islamic Studies · Computational Humanities

Quranic Stylometric NLP Pipeline

Machine learning pipeline applying stylometric analysis to Quranic Arabic — authorship attribution, rhetorical structure detection, and intertextual cross-referencing. Intersects computational linguistics and Islamic manuscript tradition. Feeds directly into the RAG pipeline as a corpus source.

AI · Fine-tuning · Low-Resource Languages

Fine-tuned LLM on Niche Corpus

Fine-tuned language model on classical Arabic poetry and Akkadian fragments — two severely underrepresented languages in existing training sets. Published to Hugging Face with full training documentation and bias analysis.

LLM wrapper targeting MENA legal and business documents. Handles dialect variation, formal register, and jurisdiction-specific terminology. Addresses a real gap in the Arabic-speaking professional market with no comparable Arabic-language tooling at accessible price points.

LLM Output Evaluation Framework

Lightweight, model-agnostic evaluation framework for scoring LLM outputs on faithfulness, relevance, and toxicity. Addresses one of the most actively unsolved problems in production AI deployment. Modular architecture designed for easy integration.

Linguistics · Gender & Language · Community Tools

Arabic Neopronoun System (انهف)

Linguistically coherent Arabic neopronoun framework designed for community use. Grammatically rigorous, culturally considered, and published for MENA LGBTQ+ communities as a practical linguistic tool. Incorporates classical Arabic morphology to ensure grammatical compatibility with existing language use.

Synthetic Biology · CRISPR · Vegan Nutrition

Chromosomal B12 Operon Integration in E. coli

Research proposal for stable B12 biosynthesis in E. coli Nissle 1917 via CRISPR. Proposes Lactobacillus reuteri as the optimal organism for replicating herbivore B12 synthesis pathways. Currently under community review at Reclone.org.
